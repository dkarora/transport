GreenDOT is the Massachusetts Department of Transportation’s bold new sustainability initiative.  GreenDOT will establish MassDOT as a national leader in promoting sustainability in the transportation sector. GreenDOT promotes the healthy transportation modes of walking, bicycling and transit.

Moving Together 2011 will provide you with the latest insight and information on the GreenDOT policy and on how these healthy transportation modes are quickly transforming the Massachusetts landscape.

Jeffrey B. Mullan, Massachusetts Secretary of Transportation and CEO, will provide the conference keynote highlighting what GreenDOT means to transportation in the Commonwealth
