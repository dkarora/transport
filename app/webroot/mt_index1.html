Please register now for Moving Together 2011, the Commonwealth's annual statewide walking and bicycling conference.  

When:   Wednesday, October, 12, 2011 8:30am - 4:00pm

Where:   Boston Sheraton Hotel
             39 Dalton Street
              Boston, MA

Who:  All parties interested in advancing bicycle and pedestrian transportation!
Moving Together is the venue to collaborate, learn, advocate and network on healthy transportation in the Commonwealth.

Attendees include local & state officials, community leaders & board members, planners, transportation engineers, landscape architects, public health & environmental professionals, advocates, educators and consultants who are all focused on bicycle and pedestrian transportation.
